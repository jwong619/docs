## <div className=""><span>Funds (Managed via Limits API)</span></div>

<b>Funds</b> act as predefined budgets you assign to employees, teams, or
initiatives:

<ul className="lists">
  <li>
    They can include merchant and category restrictions, as well as expiration
    controls
  </li>
  <li>They can be shared across multiple users or assigned individually</li>
  <li>
    Examples: "Marketing Q2 Budget", "Engineering Team Offsite", "Office
    Supplies - Austin"
  </li>
</ul>
